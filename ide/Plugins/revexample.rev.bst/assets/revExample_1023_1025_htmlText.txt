<p>This stack demonstrates some of the messages that Revolution plugins receive.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Try continuing your normal working routine with this stack open, and watch it get various environmental messages.</p>
<p></p>
<p>For example, whenever you select a new control Revolution sends "revSelectedObjectChanged" to the plugin.</p>
<p></p>
<p>You can control which messages are sent to a plugin using the Plugin Settings dialog.</p>
