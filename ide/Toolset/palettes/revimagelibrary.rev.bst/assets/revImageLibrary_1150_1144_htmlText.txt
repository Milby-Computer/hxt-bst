<p>Scripted button. Allows resizing of palette mode stacks on which setting the resizable property has no effect.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The resizer will resize the stack horizontally and vertically within the minWidth, maxWidth, minHeight and maxHeight properties set on the stack.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Note that this control requires the "Revolution Icons" image library to be tranferred to your stack using Distribution Builder when you are preparing your stack for distribution.</p>
