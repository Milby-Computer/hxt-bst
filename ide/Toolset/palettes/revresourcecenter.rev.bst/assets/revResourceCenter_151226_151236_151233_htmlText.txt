<p><font face="Lucida Grande" size="12" color="#000000">Really Simple Syndication, more commonly known by it's acronym RSS, is a format used to send web feeds. Written in XML, RSS feeds are easy to parse and can be searched quickly for items of interest. They are generally used to send summaries of news articles or web forum posts which can be displayed to the user in an easy to read format, providing them with the quick overview they desire and a link they can click if they want to read more.</font></p>
