<p>Applications in Revolution are known as stacks and the separate screens which make these up are called cards.  A stack can contain any number of cards and may also contain substacks.  The substacks will generally provide specialist actions or implement specific parts of your application.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Objects can be placed on cards by dragging them from the Toolbar.  Once an object has been placed on a card it can be moved around by further drag and drop actions or by using the objects property inspector.</p>
